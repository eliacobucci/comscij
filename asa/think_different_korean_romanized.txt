Michinnomdeul-ege geonbae. Banyeokjadeul-ege. 
Munjereul mandeuneun saram-deul-ege.
Sagagin gumeonge dumgeun mottongeul bageunneun saram-deul-ege.
Ildeureul dalleutt geu-ange boneun saram-deul-ege.

Geudeuleun gyuchigeul johahaji anhneunda.
Geuligo hyeonsang-e daehan jongyeongeul gatji anhneunda.

Dangsineneun geudeuleul chingchanhalgodo, 
bandeahalgodo, inyo-halgodo, 
midji analgodo, yeonggwang-eul jugodo,
bimanhalgodo halsu itda. Yuilhage halsu eomneun geos-eun
geudeuleul mushiha-neun geos-ida. Waenya-hamyeon geudeuleun
ildeuleul bakkugi ttaemun-ida.

Geudeuleun balmyeonghanda. Geudeuleun sangsanghanda.
Geudeuleun chiyuhanda. Geudeuleun tamguhanda.
Geudeuleul changjohanda. Geudeuleun yeonggameul junda.
Geudeuleun in-ganeul apeu-lo naagageunda.

Amado geudeul-eun mich-eo-ya handa. Eotteoke bin
kaenbeoseul bogo yesul jagpum-eul bol su ittgeolkka?
Amudo seoisseo ajik sseujil anheun nolaeleul deulel su
ittkka? Ppalgan haengseongeul bogo baqwidallin
silheomshil-eul bol su ittkka?
