Pagalon ke liye yeh hai. Vidroheyon ke liye. 
Samasya khadi karne walon ke liye. 
Gol chhedon mein chaukor khunton ke liye. 
Jo cheezon ko alag tarike se dekhte hain.

Ve niyamon ke shaukeen nahin hain. 
Aur unka yathasthiti ke liye koi sammaan nahin hai.

Aap unki prashansa kar sakte hain, unse asahmat ho sakte hain, 
unka hawala de sakte hain, un par avishwaas kar sakte hain, 
unki mahima ya ninda kar sakte hain. Keval ek cheez jo aap nahin 
kar sakte vah hai unhen nazarandaz karna. Kyonki ve cheezon ko 
badal dete hain.

Ve avishkar karte hain. Ve kalpana karte hain. Ve changa karte hain. 
Ve khojte hain. Ve banate hain. Ve prerana dete hain. 
Ve maanav jaati ko aage badhate hain.

Shayad unhen pagal hona padta hai. Bhala khaali canvas ko dekhkar 
kalakriti kaise dikhai de sakti hai? Ya maunata mein baithkar aisa 
geet kaise sunaai de sakta hai jo kabhi likha hi nahin gaya? Ya laal 
grah ko dekhkar pahiyon waali prayogshala kaise dikhai de sakti hai?
